Privacy Policy – TPC Match Tracker

Last updated: 13 April 2026

TPC Match Tracker (“the App”) is developed and maintained by Team Paixão e Competência.

Data Collection

TPC Match Tracker does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All information entered by the user (including team names, player details, match statistics, and notes) is stored locally on the user’s device only. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to the developer or any external parties.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection to function. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Third‑Party Services

The App does not use third‑party services, including analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting tools.

Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be permanently removed by uninstalling the App from the device.
